⚠️ Not Financial, Tax or Legal Advice
NHS Finance Tool is not authorised or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). Nothing on this site is financial, tax or legal advice, and nothing here is a personal recommendation. Every calculator produces an estimate for general information only. Always consult a qualified, FCA-authorised financial adviser or a qualified accountant before making decisions about your pension, tax position or company structure.

1. About This Site

NHS Finance Tool is a free set of calculators built specifically for NHS pay structures and the NHS pension. It's part of the same family of independent UK financial tools as Cookie-Free.co.uk. Nothing on this site constitutes financial, tax or legal advice.

2. Data We Collect

Calculator inputs: every calculation runs entirely inside your browser. We do not transmit, store or process any of the salary, pension or company figures you enter. No account or sign-up is required for any tool on this site.

Cookies: we do not use tracking, advertising or analytics cookies. A single browser storage item remembers that you've seen the disclaimer and cookie notice — nothing personal, and it never leaves your device.

3. NHS Pension & Tax Figures

Tax, National Insurance, NHS pension and Annual Allowance figures are based on published HMRC and NHS Pensions rates for each tax year selected. Results are estimates and may differ from your actual payslip or Annual Benefit Statement, which take into account your specific circumstances, rota, and any local pay arrangements. Always verify NHS pension figures against your statement at totalrewardstatements.nhs.uk.

4. Ltd Company & Family Income Splitting

The Ltd Company Tax Optimiser illustrates possible strategies based on the figures you enter. It does not constitute tax or legal advice. Arrangements involving dividends to a spouse or family member are subject to HMRC's settlements legislation (ITTOIA 2005 s624) and carry real risk if they lack commercial substance. Always take specialist advice from a qualified accountant before implementing any strategy shown by this tool.

5. FCA Compliance

NHS Finance Tool is not authorised or regulated by the FCA. We do not provide regulated financial advice and do not make personal recommendations. Where we refer you to a third party (for example, an adviser-matching service), that third party is responsible for its own FCA compliance, and any such link is clearly marked.

6. Disclaimer

Calculator results are estimates based on the information you enter and simplified assumptions about tax and pension rules. They are for general guidance only and must not be relied upon for financial, tax or legal decisions. Always seek advice from a qualified, regulated professional before acting.
