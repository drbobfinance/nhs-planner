Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
✅ The short version: UK-Debt.info does not collect, store or share any personal data about its visitors. There are no cookies, no tracking, and no advertising.

What Data We Collect

UK-Debt.info collects no personal data from visitors. We do not use cookies. We do not use any tracking technologies. We do not run any advertising. We do not use Google Analytics or any other analytics platform.

LocalStorage

Some pages on UK-Debt.info use your browser's localStorage to save settings — specifically, the figures entered via the admin panel on the Debt Clock page. This is stored entirely within your own browser on your own device. It is:

  • Never sent to our servers or any third party
  • Never used to identify or track you
  • Only accessible by you on your own device
  • Deletable at any time by clearing your browser data

localStorage is not a cookie and does not require consent under UK PECR regulations as it involves no tracking, profiling or cross-site data sharing.

Server Logs

UK-Debt.info is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may retain standard web server logs (IP addresses, pages visited, timestamps) for security and performance purposes in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not access or use these logs for marketing or user profiling. For details, see Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Third-Party Services

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log requests for fonts in accordance with their privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Chart.js is loaded from cdnjs.cloudflare.com. This is a static file delivery service and does not track users.

No Cookies

UK-Debt.info uses no cookies of any kind — not essential, analytical, functional or marketing cookies. No cookie consent banner is required or displayed.

Children

This site is a public information resource suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from any person of any age.

Your Rights

As we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing to access, correct or delete. If you have concerns about data held by Cloudflare regarding your visit, please refer to Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Donations (Future)

If a donation facility is added to this site in the future, payments will be processed by a third-party provider (such as Ko-fi or Stripe). Any such payments will be governed by that provider's own privacy policy. We will update this policy when a donation facility is introduced.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The date of the most recent update is shown at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy on this site, please contact: info@uk-debt.info
