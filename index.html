ℹ️ About This Site

What is UK-Debt.info?

UK-Debt.info is an independent, non-partisan public information resource providing real-time and historical data on the United Kingdom's fiscal position. It brings together official data from ONS, OBR, HM Treasury and the Bank of England in one place.

All figures are derived from official UK government sources including the Office for National Statistics (ONS), the Office for Budget Responsibility (OBR), HM Treasury, and the Bank of England. Live counters are estimates based on the latest published annual figures divided to a per-second rate — they are illustrative of the scale and direction of public finances, not audited accounts.

This site has no political affiliation. It does not advocate for any party, policy position or ideology. The data speaks for itself.

For journalists and researchers: All source data is publicly available from the links below. Please cite as "UK-Debt.info, based on ONS/OBR data" and include the date of access as figures update monthly.
