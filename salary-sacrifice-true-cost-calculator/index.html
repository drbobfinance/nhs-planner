🚗 Salary Sacrifice True Cost Calculator

Car lease, cycle to work, tech and childcare schemes save you tax today — but what do they really cost your NHS pension?

Guidance only — not financial advice. This is a simplified lifetime estimate based on the figures you enter, not a substitute for a full pension projection.
Why this matters: most NHS trusts advertise the tax and National Insurance saving from salary sacrifice schemes — and that saving is real. What they don't usually mention is that the NHS 2015 Pension Scheme is a Career Average (CARE) scheme: your pension is built up from your actual pensionable pay each year. Reduce your salary for a car lease or a bike, and you permanently reduce the pension earned in those years too — it doesn't catch up later. This calculator weighs the tax saving against that lifetime pension cost, so you can see the real trade-off rather than just the headline saving.
