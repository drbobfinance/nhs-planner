Nature of the Data

All figures displayed on UK-Debt.info are estimates derived from publicly available official data sources. They are not audited accounts and should not be treated as such. Real-time and live-ticking figures are mathematical extrapolations — calculated by dividing the most recently published annual borrowing or spending figures by the number of seconds in a year — and are intended to illustrate the scale and direction of UK public finances. They are not precise measurements of debt or spending at any given moment.

Figures are updated manually following each monthly release from the Office for National Statistics (ONS) and other official sources. Between updates, displayed numbers may lag the most current published data.
