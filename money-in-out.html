TOTAL MANAGED EXPENDITURE
All public sector spending. 44.8% of GDP.
£1,368bn
Deficit: £133bn
🚨 The structural problem: The UK now spends more on debt interest (£110bn) than on defence (£62bn) and transport (£43bn) combined. Disability and health benefit spending (£76.9bn) is growing at 10%+ per year and is the fastest-growing major spending item — driven by PIP caseload growth. Total welfare at £334bn now exceeds the entire NHS budget.
